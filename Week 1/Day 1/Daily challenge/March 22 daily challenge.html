What is League of Legends?

League of legends is a Moba game. Moba stands for Multiplayer online battle arena, which is a subgenre of strategy video games. Each player controls a single character as part of a team competing against another team of players. The ultimate objective is to destroy the opposing team's main structure with the assistance of periodically-spawned computer-controlled units that march forward along set paths.
